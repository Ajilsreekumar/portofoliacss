Learn About Me

Mark Zuckerberg is a renowned technology entrepreneur and philanthropist, best known for co-founding and leading Facebook. Born on May 14, 1984, in White Plains, New York, Zuckerberg developed an interest in computers and programming at a young age. He launched Facebook in 2004 while attending Harvard University, and the platform quickly gained popularity, expanding to become a global social networking giant. Zuckerberg has also co-founded and invested in other companies, including Instagram and WhatsApp, and has pledged to give away 99% of his Facebook shares to charity through the Chan Zuckerberg Initiative, which he established with his wife, Priscilla Chan.
